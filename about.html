ABOUT US

Who we Are

Marah Farms is the first organic and sustainable farm in Sierra Leone. The Marah's are a family of farmers and our ancestors have been farming for generations. In 2012, Morris Marah established Marah Farms, increasing the crop diversity and transitioning the farm into an organic producer.

Marah Farms is located in a diverse lanscape on the foot of the Kabala mountains. The property is 150 acres in size and we strive to effectively use all resources while seeking to maintain the correct balance between people, animals, farming and nature. We have also have a natural river that flows through the property making it deal for grazing and relaxing.
